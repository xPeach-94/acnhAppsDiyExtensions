How to Use:

Fill in the amount of materials you want to craft and what material you want to craft with.
Click "Add" if you want to add more materials.
When you noted all materials click "Calculate".
Depending on how many materials you've noted, this may take some time.
When it's finished you see all the DIY's that can be crafted with the given materials and how much profit you'll get for crafting and selling the item.
